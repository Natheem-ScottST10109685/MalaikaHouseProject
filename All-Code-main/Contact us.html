Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Malaika House and our services

How do I know if Malaika House is right for my child?

+

The best way is to book a visit! We encourage families to experience our environment firsthand. Our team will discuss your child's needs and help determine if our approach aligns with your goals.

Do you offer financial assistance or payment plans?

+

Yes, we believe our programs should be accessible. We offer sliding scale fees and payment plans based on family circumstances. Please contact us confidentially to discuss options.

Can parents be involved in sessions and programs?

+

Absolutely! Family partnership is core to our approach. We encourage parent involvement through observations, joint sessions, regular meetings, and family support resources.

What age groups do you serve?

+

We serve learners from age 3 to 18+ across different programs. Our Heart Program accommodates various age groups with age-appropriate activities and support levels.

Do you provide assessments for neurodivergent learners?

+

We conduct relationship-based assessments to understand each learner's strengths and needs. For formal diagnostic assessments, we work with partner professionals and can provide referrals.

What is the difference between your programs?

+

Our Heart Program is our comprehensive membership offering, while club programs focus on specific activities. Malaika House Sessions offer flexible individual or small group support options.
